## Ingredients

- 1 cup yellow moong dal (or toor dal), rinsed
- 3 cups water
- 1 medium tomato, chopped
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- Salt to taste

**Tadka**

- 2 tbsp ghee (or oil)
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 2 dried red chilies
- 1/2 tsp kashmiri chili powder
- Handful of cilantro, chopped

## Method

1. Combine dal, water, tomato, turmeric, and 1 tsp salt in a pressure cooker or pot. Pressure cook for 3 whistles (or simmer covered 25–30 mins) until soft. Whisk to a smooth consistency. Adjust water for desired thickness.

2. For the tadka, heat ghee in a small pan over medium-high heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter, about 30 seconds. Add garlic and fry until golden, 1–2 minutes. Add dried chilies and chili powder, stir for 10 seconds.

3. Pour the tadka immediately over the dal. Top with cilantro. Serve with rice or roti.

---

**Notes:** The dal thickens as it sits — add a splash of water and reheat if needed.
