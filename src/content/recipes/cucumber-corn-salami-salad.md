## Ingredients

- 1 large cucumber, sliced
- 1/2 cup sweet corn - boiled (fresh, frozen, or canned)
- 1 small onion, sliced
- 6–8 slices chicken salami, cut into semicircles or quaters
- 3 spring onions, chopped
- Small handful of fresh dill, roughly chopped

**Dressing**

- 3 tbsp Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 tsp mustard sauce
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- Salt to taste
- Juice of half a lemon

## Method

1. Whisk together all dressing ingredients in a small bowl. Taste and adjust salt, pepper, or lemon.

2. Combine cucumber, corn, onion, salami, spring onion, and dill in a large bowl.

3. Pour dressing over and toss to coat. Serve immediately or chill for 15 minutes to let the flavors come together.
